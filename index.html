What Cuehold does

A place for messy inputs before they become commitments.

Cuehold helps you catch loose inputs, make sense of them, and decide what should happen next.

Capture the thing

Save a messy thought, pasted message, idea, task, note, link, or screenshot before it disappears.

Make it reviewable

Keep captures in one saved-item library instead of losing them across Photos, messages, notes, and memory.

Sort what it is

Triage captures as task, idea, note, reminder, calendar, or reference so each item has a clearer shape.

Surface what matters

Use Today Signal and Up Next to bring important captures back into view when they need a decision.

See Apple context

Show Calendar and Reminder context for today without turning Cuehold into another calendar or reminder list.

Choose the next action

Decide whether to do it, schedule it, make it reminder-ready, defer it, or keep it as reference.

How Cuehold is different

Before Calendar. Before Reminders.

Calendar tells you where your time is going. Reminders tells you what you committed to. Cuehold helps decide what something is before it becomes either.

Cuehold asks: what is this, does it matter, and what should I do next? It should not duplicate everything across Apple Calendar and Reminders.
