Cuehold Support

Cuehold is an early iPhone beta focused on capture, triage, and deciding what to do next.

What it is

A capture and triage layer for messy inputs, built to work with Apple Reminders and Apple Calendar.

What it is not

Not a medical app, not a Siri replacement, not a generic AI chatbot, and not a fully automated scheduler.

Need help?
